# log.md - operations journal

The agent's readable record of what `refine` and `audit` did and why: how many inbox entries a
refine pass synthesized, which pages it touched, what it superseded, and what gaps or
contradictions it surfaced. It complements git history - git is the authoritative record of
commits; this is the narrative of decisions and the on-system home for off-page history (see
`RULEBOOK.md`, "Page layering" and "`log.md`").

Written only by refine and audit - capture does not write here. One narrative entry per
committed pass, appended newest at the bottom.
